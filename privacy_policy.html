Privacy Policy

Tactics of the Generals ("the App") is a single-player offline game. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All gameplay (board state, piece placement, match progress) is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server.

Local Storage

The App stores a small amount of data locally on your device only:

  • Your sound/music mute preference.
  • Whether you have purchased "Remove Ads" (is_ads_removed flag), so ads stay disabled after purchase.

None of this local data is shared with us or any third party.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Unless you purchase the one-time "Remove Ads" upgrade, the App displays banner and interstitial advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, and usage data to serve and measure ads, and may use this data to personalize ads in accordance with your device's ad settings. This collection and use of data is governed by Google's own privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device's ad settings (e.g. Android Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

The "Remove Ads" purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment information; Google's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App itself collects no personal information, this applies regardless of the player's age; ad content shown to known child audiences is subject to Google AdMob's applicable child-directed treatment settings configured for this app in the Play Console.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
